Bartender's Choice

This handy dandy tool helps bartenders when they are feeling uninspired and are faced with those two dreaded words–surprise me. Simply ask your guest whether they have a preference for base spirit, or if they're feeling like a certain type of drink–spirit forward, fruity & refreshing, herby and bitter, etc. etc.–then type in an ingredient that fits with their reply. After that, it's the bartender's choice! If you really can't handle decision making (we've all been there), we've got you. Simply click the choose for me button.
